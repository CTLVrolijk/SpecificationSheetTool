Focus Materials screening

Answer Yes/Possible/No for each Focus Material category. Any "Yes" or "Possible" routes the stream to Appendix A or E.

i
Why this matters
R2v3 Standard p.7 defines six Focus Material categories. Streams containing any FM are R2 Controlled and must be managed per Appendix A, E, or F. Appendix E(6) further requires FM removal prior to shredding or materials recovery.

i
B3011 vs A3210 vs Y48
REC v1.2 added Basel-controlled plastic waste entries Y48 and A3210 as R2 Controlled Streams. B3011 (non-hazardous plastic, sorted by single resin, low contamination) remains unrestricted. BFR-containing plastic is A3210; mixed plastic failing B3011 purity criteria is Y48.
